Good Shipping的优势

Good Shipping创立于上海经济特区临港自由贸易试验区, 毗邻全球航运枢纽，世界著名大港-洋山深水港以及洋山综合保税区，公司享有特殊的海运经济贸易优惠政策和制度优势。

Good Shipping was founded in the Shanghai Special Economic Zone Lingang Free Trade Pilot Zone, adjacent to the global shipping hub, the world-renowned Yangshan Deepwater Port, and the Yangshan Comprehensive Bonded Zone. The company enjoys special maritime economic and trade preferential policies and institutional advantages.

Good Shipping是国家一级货运代理和NVOCC资质的综合性国际航运和国际物流企业，通用Good Shipping名义服务于海内外客户。

Good Shipping is a comprehensive international shipping and logistics enterprise with national first-class freight forwarding and NVOCC qualifications, serving customers both domestically and internationally under the name of Good Shipping.

国内
DOMESTIC

Good Shipping船务服务总部位于上海，并拥有青岛、天津、宁波、广州等全资子公司，目前以上海为中心，提供辐射长三角、珠三角的国际海运订舱、陆运、仓储、报关、报检、集装箱货物承揽、国际物流等一站式服务。

Good Shipping International Shipping is headquartered in Shanghai and has wholly-owned subsidiaries such as Qingdao, Tianjin, Ningbo, and Guangzhou. Currently, with Shanghai as the center, it provides one-stop services such as international sea freight booking, land transportation, warehousing, customs declaration, inspection, container cargo contracting, and international logistics that radiate to the Yangtze River Delta and Pearl River Delta.
